About Dr. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, fondly known as the Missile Man of India, was a renowned scientist, visionary leader, and the 11th President of India. He dedicated his life to making India self-reliant in defense, space, and nuclear technology.

Life Highlights

  • 1931: Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1963: Joined ISRO, developing India's first satellite launch vehicle.
  • 1980s: Led missile development under IGMDP.
  • 1998: Played a key role in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
  • 2002: Became the 11th President of India.
  • 2015: Passed away inspiring the youth with his lecture.

Legacy and Contributions

Known for his passion for education, Dr. Kalam inspired millions with his books, speeches, and dedication to innovation. He remains an eternal source of inspiration for dreamers and achievers worldwide.
